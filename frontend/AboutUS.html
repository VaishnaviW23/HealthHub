About Health Hub

Who We Are?

Health Hub is a smart healthcare platform designed to connect patients, doctors and hospitals through a single digital system.

Our Mission

To make healthcare services easily accessible, improve communication between patients and doctors, and provide quick assistance during emergencies.

Key Features

  • 📅 Online Appointment Booking
  • 📂 Health Record Management
  • 🤖 AI Health Assistant Chatbot
  • 🚨 Emergency Support
  • 🏥 Hospital Information & Maps
  • 👨‍⚕️ Doctor & Patient Dashboards
